# ⚽ FootureX

**Football. Future. X.**
A next-generation platform for building a digital football ecosystem.

---

## 🚀 About

FootureX is a football simulator and management platform that connects players, teams, and leagues into a unified digital environment.
The project focuses on long-term ecosystem growth rather than short-term monetization.

---

## 🎮 Core Features

* Team creation and management
* Player progression and customization
* Competitive leagues and tournaments
* Multi-currency in-game economy
* Ranking and achievement systems

---

## 💰 Ecosystem

FootureX introduces a structured in-game economy:

* In-game Coins
* Stars
* TON
* Native project token

The system is designed to maintain balance and ensure sustainable growth.

---

## 🏆 Roadmap Vision

* Tournaments with real prize pools
* Expansion to international markets
* Blockchain integration
* Continuous updates and new game modes
